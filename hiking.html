HIKING, BIKING AND
MEHAREE

North Africa is characterized by its attractive regions from its balmy coastline to the remote landscapes of its interior and long-varied mountains. This is what makes it fertile ground for trekking, biking and Meharee tours.


You will visit remote and non-touristic villages, summiting peaks, wandering into the Moroccan hills and zigzag along the wild trails that are decorated with incredible scenery, ride Camel ( Meharee) to discover the gigantic dunes , the silence of the sahara, the magic of it vast dunes and enjoy the spectacular sunrise & sunset …., all while learning interesting facts from your guide, who will provide you with an authentic experience during your hike and will leave you with countless stories to tell .


Our designed trips include the four mountain ranges of Morocco: The High Atlas which serves as a natural barricade between the Sahara Desert in the South and the Atlantic Ocean.


The Middle Atlas and is known by Jebel Toubkal, the first summit in North Africa vaults 167. There is also M’Goun Massif 4071 m. This lets you spend more time savoring the drama of the mountain scenery and valleys that are home to the local Berber tribes


The Anti Atlas and is known by Jebel Saghro The highest peak, Amalou n'Mansour at 2712m, so the risk of altitude sickness is generally less of a problem. There is also Jebel Sirwa 3304-metre-high volcanic mountain.


The Anti-Atlas is a much less heralded mountain range than the High Atlas and lies in the south of Morocco, but it still boasts a number of tempting peaks and the Rif Mountains in the North of Morocco.


The area is at its best in late spring with carpets of wild flowers and dramatic snow-melt rivers in valleys.
