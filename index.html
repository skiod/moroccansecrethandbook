OUR
STORY

MSH is a fully licensed company specialised in tailor-made travel throughout Morocco. Whether you travel on a budget, or expect only the most upscale arrangements, MSH offers exclusive, fully customised experiences and itineraries designed and delivered by a team of multi-lingual professionals with a first-hand knowledge of the hidden gems of Morocco and its most inspiring secrets, that they are proud to share with their guests.


MSH was founded in 2017 by Ms Loubna, a Berber woman born and raised in the Sahara. Her father was a well-educated and open-minded man, and encouraged her to study. She took a degree in Travel and Tourism and completed a Master in English literature before becoming a travel designer in 2002, working for the most prestigious DMCs in Morocco and developing a network of loyal clients all over the world, many of them relevant members of Virtuoso and other well-respected travel networks. An expert in Berber folklore and traditions, Loubna escorted research groups from the University of Nothingham for several years across the Atlas Mountains, assisting them in their study of Berber traditional medicine and the local use of plants for the concoction of natural remedies.  Travel is her passion; she has a deep knowledge of Morocco and in addition she has visited more than 40 foreign countries...
