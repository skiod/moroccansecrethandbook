SHOPPING AND
FASHION

Sitting pretty at North Africa and very close to Europe, Morocco boasts of some truly magnificent shopping options that will make any shopaholic get into a frenzy.


The old city of Fes and Marrakech and the huge south of Morocco hold a fascinating mix of old and new — making it the perfect destination to stock up on traditional arts and crafts as well as modern fashion.


With the help of your guide, you discover fabulous new finds to add to your wardrobe or home. There is possibility to Enjoy a one-on-one personal shopping experience if you wish.


Culture Scouts walking tour is designed to make you learn and engage with Morocco's diverse art and culinary scene. This will provide you with each city's cultural hubs in a capsule experience blending art, history, retail and guided conversation with the local.


The itinerary traverse routes of galleries, cafes, shops and bars, led by tour guides who share their diverse knowledge in sociable and thought-prvoking tour sessions.
