VINEYARD AND
WINE HISTORY

Get off the beaten path and set off for a few hours, to discover the world of wine in Morocco...


Although most Moroccans do not drink alcohol due to religious prohibitions, Morocco has been a leading wine producer for several years and its bold red and white grapes have become popular among groups of ex-patriots and foreigners from France, England, America, Spain, Germany and Italy who enjoy healthy alcohol consumption.


Our Wine-to-Wine tour includes a stunning vineyard nestled between the foothills of the Rif and the Middle Atlas mountains. We will make you learn the whole wine-making process, from grape growing in the fields to wine-making in the cellar and wine tasting.


Culture Scouts walking tour is designed to make you learn and engage with Morocco’s diverse art and culinary scene. This will provide you with each city’s cultural hubs in a capsule experience blending art, history, retail and guided conversation with the local.


The farm also produces an award-winning olive oil – and not just any award: Volubilia Extra Virgin gained the title Best Olive Oil in the World in 2006. During the tour , you enjoy a traditional Moroccan lunch at the winery.
