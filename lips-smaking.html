Morocco is a rich country by its heritage and cultural divers and its gastronomy, which recognized as one of the most refined in the world.

The old city of Fes and Marrakech and the huge south of Morocco hold a fascinating mix of old and new — making it the perfect destination to stock up on traditional arts and crafts as well as modern fashion.


It is the result of a combination of many cultures coming from different horizons: Berber, Arabo-Muslim, Jewish, Andalusian and Mediterranean. Moroccan cuisine owes its success to its ability to blend aromas and spices into unique flavors.


Moroccan meals, drinks, breads and cakes will certainly give you a different taste of the country.
