WOMEN
SOLO

We design tours in which we bring solo female together on Journey filled with fun experiences , adventure, exploration and Pure relaxation. All in a safe environment where you feel like you belong.


Our tours for women-only are carefully curated to suit the taste of an adventurous female traveler.


You will experience the cultural traditions of women in the region, share with them their stories , enjoy eclectic and delicious cuisine & wine, fabulous company, culture, nature, wildlife , enjoy fascinating landscapes straight out of Arabian Nights, stay in the warm Sahara sand and watch the sun dip below the dunes, sleep under the stars in Bedouin camps, sweet tea by the bucket load and hospitality that warms the soul ; an opportunity to break down barriers and preconceived notions,– all this and much more awaits you when you go travelling to Morocco.


Our trips are designed by women, for women and are led by women.


Safety:

If you want to travel as a woman in Morocco , whether alone or with a group , you actually have nothing to worry about , as long as you follow certain customs and rules of conduct. Just like everywhere else in the world, it is possible to fall in the wrong people.

Still, don’t let yourself be swayed by sweeping judgements about how safe the region is . Be mindful, careful and vigilant, but not paranoid.
